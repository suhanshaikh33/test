Welcome to Athar Technical LLC

ABCO Steel International Pvt. Ltd. is one of the leading steel trading companies in India. The company is having its major operation in Udupi, Mangalore & Hassan. The company deals in all kinds of steel products starting from the smallest steel pipes to massive structural beams and thick steel sheets, etc. The company has been serving the steel products needs of various industrial commercial, civil constructions, infrastructural & engineering projects. Being an ISO 9001 company, ABCO Steel works in a systematic way in handling the requirements of every customer.
